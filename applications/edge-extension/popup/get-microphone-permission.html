Permission request

In order to use the microphone, you will need to allow the extension to do so.

When prompted you can allow the microphone or use the browser microphone icon to configure the permission.

Remove the permissions

If you change your mind, use the contextual menu on the chatbot and select microphone.

On this page, use your browser microphone icon to remove the permission.

As a reminder, you will not be able to use the microphone afterwards.

What happen to your audio data?

When you interact with your voice on the chatbot, the sample is sent to our Microsoft Speech To Text instance for transcription.

The samples are not recorded or kept for any purposes.
